# 🐺 PRO TRADING OPERATING PROTOCOL

---

## 1️⃣ Pre-Market (15-20 min)

- Determine HTF Bias (4H/1H)
- Identify liquidity zones
- Identify Kill Zones
- **Don't look for trades**
- **Don't add expectations**

**Output:** Clear Bias + Zones = Done
**If unclear → No trading day**

---

## 2️⃣ Activation Filter

Before any entry, verify 3:

1. Inside Kill Zone
2. Clear Sweep
3. Displacement / BOS

**Missing ONE → Done**

---

## 3️⃣ Execution Logic

**Entry ONLY if:**

- Retrace to FVG/OB
- RRR ≥ 2.0
- SL fixed ≤ limit
- Fixed size (3 contracts)
- Pending order or calculated entry

**Forbidden:**
- Random market entry
- Moving SL
- Adding contracts

---

## 4️⃣ Trade Management

**After entry:**
- Don't touch trade
- Don't move SL
- Don't close from fear
- Don't add contracts

**Either TP or SL. Nothing in between.**

---

## 5️⃣ Daily Limit

- One clean trade = enough
- Two trades = max
- 2 losses → stop immediately
- 1 win → day done

---

## 6️⃣ Discipline Protocol (30 days)

❌ No development
❌ No rule changes
❌ No new indicators
❌ No new markets
❌ No new strategies

**Development = after 30 days only**

---

## 7️⃣ OpenClaw Role

**NOT:**
- Analyst
- Signal provider

**IS:**
- Checks conditions
- Prevents breaks
- Applies max loss limit
- Rejects outside zone

**= Security system. Not trading brain.**

---

## 8️⃣ Weekly Evaluation (once/week)

- How many trades followed system?
- How many broke rules?
- Was size fixed?
- Was RRR fixed?

**No daily emotional evaluation.**

---

## 🎯 Golden Rule

> **Professionalism = Simple repetition with discipline**
> 
> Not intelligence.
> Not complex analysis.
> Not news reading.
> 
> **Execute same idea 100 times without change.**
