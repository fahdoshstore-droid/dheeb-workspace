## Risk Management Rules

### Per Trade
- Risk: 1-2% max
- RRR: ≥ 2:1 (preferably 3:1)

### Daily
- Max Loss: 2% ($1,000)
- Stop at 2 losing trades

### Weekly
- Review + adjust
- Goal: +2-5%

---

## Key Prop Firm Insights

### From Research

> "Stick to consistent, rule-compliant strategies with clear risk management"

> "Discipline is the currency of kings"

> "Craft a rulebook: Enter on confluences, exit at 1:3 risk-reward"
