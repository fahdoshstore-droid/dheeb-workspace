# ICT Concepts - Key Learnings (March 2026)

## Order Block - Valid Requirements

**Order Block must have ALL of:**
1. Sweep liquidity ✅
2. Create imbalance ✅
3. Stay untouched ✅
4. Cause structure break ✅

---

## Order Block + FVG

- If liquidity is NEAR the OB (price reaches OB before liquidity) → **Lower credibility**
- If FVG aligns with OB → **Higher probability**

---

## FVG (Fair Value Gap)

**Key levels to watch:**
- Start of FVG
- End of FVG
- 50% midpoint = "Mean Threshold" or "Consecutive Encroachment" (CE)

**CE = very significant reaction point**

---

## Liquidity Sweep Types

### Liquidity Grab
- Quick reaction at key level
- Reversal play

### Liquidity Sweep
- Price falls below/above liquidity level and comes back
- After sweep → FVG = continuation zone

---

## 5 ICT Entry Models

### 1. Original FVG
- Wait for FVG to form
- Enter when price returns to fill

### 2. Order Block
- Wait for OB to form
- Enter at OB

### 3. Breaker Block
- OB that gets broken becomes "breaker"
- Trade retest of breaker

### 4. Liquidity Sweep
- Wait for liquidity sweep
- Enter after sweep + FVG/OB

### 5. OTE (Optimal Trade Entry)
- Fibonacci 62-79%
- Best at 62%

---

## Sequence for High Probability Trade

```
1. Identify Trend (HTF)
2. Mark Liquidity Pools (PDH/PDL)
3. Wait for Liquidity Sweep
4. Look for FVG/OB formation
5. Wait for Retracement
6. Enter at FVG + OB (confluence)
7. SL below/above liquidity
```
