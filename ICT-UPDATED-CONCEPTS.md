# ICT Trading Concepts - From Latest Research

## Key Concepts (2025-2026)

### 1. FVG (Fair Value Gap)
- **Bullish FVG**: Acts as support
- **Bearish FVG**: Acts as resistance
- **First Reaction**: Price drops into gap, touches top, closes above = buyers active = continue upward
- **High Probability**: When FVG aligns with BoS or Order Block

### 2. Order Blocks + FVG Confirmation
- Use Order Block AND FVG together
- FVG confirms OB validity
- Higher probability entry

### 3. Power of Three (PO3)
1. A - At the Break
2. M - At the Retest
3. D - At the Pullback

### 4. IFVG (Inverted FVG) + Liquidity Sweeps
- Combine IFVG with liquidity sweeps
- High probability setups

### 5. Breaker Blocks
- OB that gets broken becomes Breaker Block
- Can trade retest of breaker

### 6. Silver Bullet Strategy

- 10-11 AM NY time
