## 2. 4-Pro Traders System

### Raschke
- Risk: 1% max per trade
- News blackout: 30-45 min post-news
- Friday cutoff: 2 PM EST

### Carter
- Drawdown rules:
  - 2% = reduce size
  - 4% = minimal mode
  - 8% = stop trading
- Emotion control

### Hougaard
- Psychology checks
- "Best Loser Wins"
- No revenge trading

### Wieland
- Session windows
- Flat by close
- 2 losses = stop
