# ICT Timeframes + TRIL - Complete System

## Timeframes in ICT

### 3 Levels

| Level | Timeframes | Purpose |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **HTF** | Daily, 4H | Overall direction/trend |
| **ITF** | 1H, 30m | Market structure + confirmation |
| **LTF** | 15m, 5m, 1m | Precise entry point |

---

## TRIL vs ICT Timeframes

| TRIL | ICT Timeframes |
|------|-----------------|
| T (Trend) | HTF (Daily/H4) |
| R (Liquidity) | HTF/ITF (PDH/PDL) |
| I (FVG/OB) | LTF (15m/5m) |
| L (Session) | Kill Zones |

---

## Workflow

```
1. HTF (Daily) → Identify Trend
2. ITF (H1/30m) → Confirm Structure
3. LTF (15m/5m) → Find FVG/OB + Entry
4. TRIL → Full Trade Analysis
```

---

## Complete System

### Step 1: HTF Analysis (Daily)
- Identify trend (Bullish/Bearish)
- Find PDH, PDL
- Mark liquidity levels

### Step 2: ITF Analysis (H1)
- Confirm market structure
- Look for BoS/MSS
- Identify potential OB/FVG zones

### Step 3: LTF Entry (15m/5m)
- Wait for FVG/OB to form
- Enter at optimal point
- Set tight SL

### Step 4: TRIL Check
- T: Trend confirmed?
- R: Liquidity sweep happened?
- I: FVG/OB present?
- L: Kill Zone active?
