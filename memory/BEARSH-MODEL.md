# Bearsh Model Rules

## Basic Rules

1. **Don't change bias** at Standard Deviation 2-3
2. **Wait for break** on 15min frame
3. **Confirmation** on 1min frame (franklin)

---

## Retracement Rules

- Retracement if market is bullish
- Use **50% level**
- Above 50%: liquidity will be taken
- Broken high usually retraces **more than 50%**
- Deep retracement at PM Session level: stop between **8-9 reversal**

---

## Entry Confirmation

1. Order Block identified
2. Standard Deviation confirmed
3. **Inversion** seen at highs
4. Retracing from previous day Order Block

---

## Targets

- **Target 1:** Broke the high
- **Target 2:** 2.5 equal lows

---

## Execution Rules

- Read market from **Weekly + Daily**
- Read **Asia + London** sessions
- Measure **Standard Deviation** for London + NY
- **Execution** after Break of Structure on **4H**
- Follow **EMS** on **15min - 5min**
- **Volume Profile** important at 50% of range

---

## Specific Scenarios

### When retracement goes below daily/weekly OB
- Look for **long**
- Target: Weekly Low

### Weekly Low Target
- Execution: 15min frame
- Stop: 70 or 280 ticks
- On Mini: Stop max **$140**

---

## Pairs & Confirmation

- Mitigation Block or Breaker: determine on **both pairs**
- Wait for **SMT divergence**
- Wait for **London session**
- **Inverted FVG** = strong signal

---

## Important Notes

- Watch out for **news days**
- If loss: $200-300 → 2 micro lots
- If contract loss doubles+: high reward but higher risk
- **Standard Deviation is excellent** for confirmation
