# ICT Macros - Full Guide

## Step 1: Preparation
- [ ] Mark Liquidity Levels (BSL + SSL)
- [ ] Identify Inefficiencies (FVG + VIB)
- [ ] Mark Structural Levels (OB + BB)
- [ ] Analysis on current + higher timeframe

## Step 2: Execution Catalyst
- [ ] Wait for Price Action:
  - Sweep liquidity (BSL/SSL)
  - Fill imbalance (FVG/VIB)
- [ ] Must happen DURING macro time

## Step 3: Entry & Confirmation
- [ ] Wait for CHoCH (Change of Character)
- [ ] Enter from FVG or BB
- [ ] Target: opposing liquidity
- [ ] SMT Divergence = bonus confirmation

## Alternative Entry (No CHoCH)
If:
1. Liquidity sweep was clear
2. FVG immediately available

Then: Enter without CHoCH

---

## Macro Types

### Accumulation
- Creates liquidity during duration
- Usually retracement
- Don't trade

### Distribution
- Price expansion in trend direction
- Preceded by liquidity sweep
- Enter at START of macro (before expansion)

### Manipulation
- Liquidity sweep in same direction as order flow
- SHOULD TRADE

## Checklist

- [ ] Within Macro Window?
- [ ] Liquidity Swept?
- [ ] Displacement happened?
- [ ] FVG identified?
- [ ] Retracing to FVG?
- [ ] Clear target (opposing liquidity)?

## Bias Rules

**Bullish:** Look for SSL sweep → expect up
**Bearish:** Look for BSL sweep → expect down
