# Fahad's Trading Journey - Lessons Learned

## Years of Mistakes & Breakthroughs

### Core Problems Identified

1. **Impatient** - rushes price action, fears retracement
2. **Lot Size Increase** - increases risk thinking of double profit
3. **No Model Following** - enters based on "market mood" not his system
4. **Unclear Entry Criteria** - doesn't know exactly what he wants
5. **No Stop Loss** - biggest mistake
6. **Emotional Trading** - wakes up and trades
7. **No Reinvestment** - profits not invested elsewhere
