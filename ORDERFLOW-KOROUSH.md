# Order Flow Masterclass - Koroush AK

## Summary

### Lesson 1: Fundamentals
- **Passive Traders**: Place limit orders (provide liquidity)
- **Aggressive Traders**: Place market orders (move price)
- **Key Insight**: Large volume ≠ Price movement. Check order book thickness!

### Lesson 2: Bookmap Interface
| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Volume Bubbles | Aggressive orders (green=buy, red=sell) |
| Heat Map | Passive orders (red/orange = thick, blue = thin) |
| DOM | Market depth |

### Lesson 3: Momentum Patterns
- Green bubble clusters in direction of move
- Trailing limit orders behind price
- Thin order book ahead = easy movement

### Lesson 4: Mean Reversion
- Thick book at level = real liquidity
- Absorption = volume hits without movement
- Decreasing volume = exhaustion

### Lesson 5: Workflow
1. Identify setup (chart-based strategy) ← PRIMARY
2. Check Bookmap for confirmation
3. Adjust conviction/size/targets

**Key: Order flow is CONFIRMATION, not primary signal**
